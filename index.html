... "Hello, World!" ...

Printing this famous line has become the symbol of possibility to first-time programming students everywhere around the globe. Its meaning is as simple as it is thrilling: no matter who you are, no matter your language, ethnicity, or background, the computer understood the instructions you gave it and executed them successfully. In the Information era the skill of translating vision into reality efficiently and accurately is highly prized. Just like the Silk Road of ancient times, which facilitated commerce by building infrastructure, today, creating spaces for communication, exchange of ideas, and collabarative work is the key to success in the ever-changing global socioeconomic landscape.
